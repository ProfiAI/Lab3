Yahya Al Sinwar

A prominent Hamas leader and the current head of Hamas in the Gaza Strip. He was one of the founders of the Hamas military wing, the Izz ad-Din al-Qassam Brigades, and has been a key figure in the group’s operations. He was imprisoned by Israel for over 20 years before being released in a 2011 prisoner exchange
